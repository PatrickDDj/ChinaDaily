Xi made the remarks in Beijing when addressing the 12th BRICS summit via video link. China will redouble its efforts to expand domestic demand, deepen reform in all aspects, and promote innovation in science and technology to add impetus to its economic growth, he stressed. Instead of shutting its door of opening-up, China will embrace the world with more open arms, Xi said.
